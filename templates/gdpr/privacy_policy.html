Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our lead generation and management platform.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information (username, email, password)
  • Organization details
  • Payment information (processed securely via Stripe)

2.2 Lead Data

  • Business names and contact information
  • Phone numbers and email addresses
  • Location data (city, country, address)
  • Business category and ratings

2.3 Usage Data

  • How you use our platform
  • Messages sent and received
  • API calls and interactions

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Send important service updates
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Stripe (payments), hosting providers
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger or acquisition

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Export your data
  • Object: Object to processing
  • Restrict: Restrict processing

To exercise these rights, contact us or use the GDPR dashboard in your account.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Encrypted database storage
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You can request deletion at any time.

8. Cookies

We use cookies for authentication and session management. See our Cookie Policy for details.

9. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the EU. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

10. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes.
