Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide a better experience and remember your preferences.

2. How We Use Cookies

2.1 Essential Cookies

Required for the website to function:

  • Session cookies: Keep you logged in
  • Security cookies: Protect against CSRF attacks

2.2 Functional Cookies

Enhance your experience:

  • Preferences: Remember your settings
  • Language: Remember your language choice

2.3 Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how you use our site (optional):

  • Page views and navigation
  • Feature usage
  • Error tracking

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may use services that set cookies:

  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Analytics: Usage statistics (if enabled)

4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Your browser settings
  • Our cookie consent banner (when shown)
  • Opting out of analytics cookies

5. Cookie Duration

  • Session cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Remain until expiration or deletion

6. Updates

We may update this cookie policy. Check this page periodically for changes.
